About Us

Brooklyn Nine-Nine is not just a precinct; it's a family. Set in the heart of the bustling 99th precinct in Brooklyn , our beloved characters bring you a unique blend of comedy and crime-solving that has captured the hearts of fans worldwide. Led by the quirky yet brilliant Detective Jake Peralta, our team navigates the challenges of police work with a side of humor that keeps you laughing and on the edge of your seat.

Meet the Squad

From the deadpan genius of Captain Raymond Holt to the lovable goofiness of Charles Boyle, each member of the Nine-Nine brings their own flavor to the mix. Whether it's the tough-as-nails Detective Rosa Diaz, the no-nonsense Detective Amy Santiago, or the lovably clueless Detective Terry Jeffords, our squad is as diverse as Brooklyn itself.

Our Mission

At Brooklyn Nine-Nine, our mission is clear - to entertain and unite fans through laughter and suspense. With gripping cases, unexpected plot twists, and unforgettable moments of camaraderie, we strive to create a show that keeps you coming back for more.

Join for the fun

Become part of the Nine-Nine family by tuning in to our episodes. Whether you're a die-hard fan or a newcomer to the precinct, there's always room for more laughter, excitement, and a touch of the absurd.
